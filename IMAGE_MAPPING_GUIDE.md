# YGGDRILL Image Mapping Guide

This guide maps YGGDRILL WordPress image URLs to their intended locations in the Shopify theme. Since Shopify cannot directly use external URLs, these images must be uploaded to Shopify Admin.

## How to Upload Images

1. Go to **Shopify Admin > Settings > Files**
2. Upload each image
3. Use the uploaded image URL in your theme settings or product listings

## Theme Section Images

### Hero Banner
Upload a high-quality hero image showing Bitaxe miners or your product lineup.

**Recommended specs:**
- Size: 1920x1080px minimum
- Format: JPG or PNG
- File size: Under 500KB for fast loading

**Upload location:** Shopify Admin > Online Store > Themes > Customize > Hero Banner

### Category Images
For the "Shop by Category" section, upload representative images:

| Category | Suggested Image |
|----------|-----------------|
| Bitaxe Miners | Bitaxe 602 or Gamma product shot |
| Cooling Upgrades | Heatsink or thermal paste |
| Accessories | OLED display or stand |

## Notes

- All images should be optimized for web (compressed)
- Use descriptive alt text for accessibility
- Product images work best with white or transparent backgrounds
- Maintain consistent image dimensions within each product category
